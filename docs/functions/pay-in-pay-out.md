## Who this is for

- Cashiers and shift managers who record cash drawer movements during service.
- `Manager only`: some stores restrict `Pay In / Pay Out` by role and permission settings.

## Before you start

- Confirm you are signed in.
- Confirm there is an active business day.
- Confirm your store has pay-in/pay-out reasons configured.

## Steps

1. Tap `More Tools`.
2. Tap `Pay In / Pay Out`.
3. Tap `Pay In` or tap `Pay Out`.
4. Select a reason from `Select Pay In reason` or `Select Pay Out reason`.
5. Enter an amount when prompted with `Please enter amount`.
6. Review `Confirm this record?` and tap `OK`.

## What you should see

- A new record appears in the `Pay In / Pay Out` list with updated `Reason`, `Amount`, `User`, and `Time`.
- A pay-in/pay-out slip prints if slip printing is enabled in your store.

## If something goes wrong

- If you see `No active business day. Please try again later.`, stop and check day status first.
- If you see `No Pay In reasons are available` or `No Pay Out reasons are available`, reason setup is missing.
- If you see `Amount must be greater than zero`, enter a positive amount.
- If you see `Unable to save Pay In / Pay Out record`, retry once after checking network and then escalate.

## When to ask manager/admin

- You cannot access `Pay In / Pay Out` because of role restrictions.
- Reason lists are missing or incorrect.
- Save fails repeatedly or the printed slip does not match the recorded amount.
