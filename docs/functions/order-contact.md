---
title: Order Contact
---

:::info Who this is for
Cashiers and shift managers who need to save customer contact details on a takeaway order.
:::

## Before you start

- You are signed in and on `Ordering`.
- You have opened the correct takeaway order.
- You know the customer details your store is allowed to collect.

## Steps

1. Open the target takeaway order in `Ordering`.
2. Tap the function menu (three dots).
3. In `Select Function`, tap `Order Contact`.
4. If `Order Contact` is on another page, tap `Previous` or `Next`.
5. Enter the customer name in `Name` if needed.
6. Enter the customer phone number in `Phone` if needed.
7. Tap `Confirm`.
8. Check the saved contact appears under the order header.

## What you should see

- The `Order Contact` dialog shows `Name` and `Phone`.
- The saved contact appears on the takeaway order screen after you confirm.
- Receipt and kitchen printouts can include this contact when store print settings allow it.

## If something goes wrong

- If `Order Contact` is missing, confirm the order is `Takeaway` and reopen the function menu.
- If the contact does not appear under the header after `Confirm`, reopen `Order Contact` and enter the details again.
- If the contact is saved on screen but missing from printouts, check `Settings` -> `Printing` for the order-contact print toggles.

## When to ask manager/admin

- `Manager only`: your store requires approval before collecting customer phone numbers. Cashier fallback: leave the fields blank and continue the order.
- You need order contact to print, but the print setting is locked or unavailable.
- Contact details still do not print after you retry with the correct settings.
