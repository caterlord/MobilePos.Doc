# Online Ordering Overview

![Online Ordering Overview](/img/hq/online-ordering/oo_overview_page.png)

The **Online Ordering** module manages how your POS Menu catalog is projected to customer-facing channels such as BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) QR Code ordering, native Mobile Apps, and third-party API aggregators.

Rather than managing a separate database of items, Online Ordering syncs directly with your core Menu Catalog, allowing you to selectively publish items, apply different pricing tiers, and override translations specifically for the digital storefront.

## Core Modules

The dashboard is broken down into specific configuration routes:

1. **Categories & Items**: Selectively publish your POS categories to the online catalog.
2. **Online Menus**: Group your published categories into specific digital menus (e.g., a "Lunch Delivery Menu").
3. **Channel Settings**: Map your online menus to specific order ingestion points (e.g., routing Menu A to Foodpanda and Menu B to the QR Code web-app).
4. **General Settings**: Configure shop-level operational rules (pickup lead times, delivery throttles, offline toggles).
5. **Call To Action**: Configure cross-sell and up-sell prompts during the checkout flow.
6. **UI Translations**: Directly modify the string variables on the customer-facing web-app without needing a developer to deploy code.
