# General Settings (Shop Overrides)

![General Settings](/img/hq/online-ordering/oo_general_settings_page.png)

The **General Settings** module controls the operational behavior of the digital storefront *per physical shop*. 

Because a brand might have a fast-casual kiosk store in a mall and a full-service dine-in restaurant in a hotel, their online ordering variables must be governed independently.

## Shop Selection
Upon entering the module, you must explicitly **select a Shop** from the dropdown menu to load its corresponding parameters.

## Core Operations

### Service Modes
Toggle exactly which service modes this shop accepts via the digital app:
- **Takeaway (Pickup)**
- **Dine-In (QR Code)**
- **Delivery** 

If a mode is disabled here, it will instantly vanish from the customer app, even if the shop is otherwise open.

### Preparation & Lead Times
- **Default Prep Time**: Inform the customer how long their order will take (e.g., `15 minutes`). This delays the firing ticket to the kitchen if the customer scheduled an order for later in the day.
- **Maximum Advance Orders**: Configure how many days in advance a customer is allowed to schedule a pre-order.

### Emergency Toggles
If the kitchen is temporarily overwhelmed, a manager can log in here and toggle the **"Suspend Online Orders"** switch. This immediately halts incoming digital traffic to that specific shop while keeping the POS terminal operational for walk-in customers.
