# Billing & Subscriptions

![Marketplace Billing](/img/hq/marketplace/marketplace_billing_page.png)

The **Billing & Subscriptions** page tracks all the financial implications of your active Marketplace modules.

## Invoice History

You can download monthly PDFs of your HQ software licensing costs. This is useful for reconciliation.

## Managing Subscriptions

This page also lists every single active `Marketplace` module you currently subscribe to. 
If an integration is no longer needed (e.g., you discontinued your Deliveroo contract), you must navigate here to cancel the marketplace subscription to halt billing.
