# Sales Reports

![Sales Reports](/img/hq/reports/sales_reports_page.png)

The **Sales Reports** module is the financial heart of the HQ operations. It provides comprehensive, real-time analytics to measure store performance, pinpoint trends, and reconcile financials.

X1 provides over 16 distinct analytical ledger views, separated into four primary groups:

## 1. Revenue & Trends
These reports provide high-level visibility on gross and net income.
- **Sales Overview**: A dashboard-style graphical summary of today's vs last week's performance.
- **Sales Summary**: The standard ledger of Net Sales, Gross Sales, Taxes, and Service Charges over a given time period.
- **Annual Turnover**: Macro-level, year-over-year revenue comparisons.
- **Hourly Report**: Breaks down transaction volume by the hour to help you schedule staff during peak rushes.
- **Revenue Attainment**: Compares actual tracked sales against your configured budget/targets.

## 2. Breakdown Analysis
Slice the revenue data by specific operational mechanics:
- **Sales by Item**: What was the best-selling dish this month?
- **Sales by Section/Department**: Macro PnL grouping based on logical accounting categories.
- **Sales by Discount**: Tracks how much dollar value was surrendered to promotional activities.
- **Payment Methods**: Breaks down revenue by tender type (Visa, Cash, Alipay, WeChat Pay) to assist with nightly bank reconciliation.

## 3. Detailed Records
Highly tactical reports for auditing purposes:
- **Transaction List**: A raw chronological feed of every single receipt printed. You can drill down to view the actual digital receipt copy.
- **Food Cost**: An analytical spread comparing total Net Revenue against the theoretical COGS (Cost of Goods Sold).
- **Item Sold Out**: A historical log tracking when items hit their depletion limits.

## 4. Customers & Partners
- **Members**: Evaluates sales volume attributed to recognized VIPs/Loyalty members versus anonymous transactions.
